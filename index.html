About the Temple

Puttur Shree Mahalingeshwara Temple is a renowned 12th-century temple located in Puttur, Dakshina Kannada, Karnataka. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is famous for its magnificent architecture, peaceful surroundings, and centuries-old traditions.
